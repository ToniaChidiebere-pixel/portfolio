## Project Overview
This project analyzes student behavioral and academic data to understand factors influencing productivity and academic performance.

The goal is to identify lifestyle patterns that improve student outcomes using data-driven insights.


## Dataset Description
The dataset contains records of 20,000 students including:

- Study hours
- Sleep duration
- Phone usage
- Social media activity
- Attendance
- Stress level
- Final grades
- Productivity score


## Objectives
- Determine factors affecting productivity
- Analyze academic performance drivers
- Evaluate lifestyle impact on grades
- Identify performance improvement opportunities


## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook


## Key Insights

### Study Habits
Students who study longer hours generally achieve higher final grades.

Insight:
Consistent study time strongly influences academic success.


### Sleep & Productivity
Moderate sleep duration correlates with higher productivity scores.

Insight:
Sleep deprivation negatively impacts performance.


### Phone Usage
Higher phone usage hours show reduced productivity levels.

Insight:
Excessive mobile usage distracts students from academic activities.


### Attendance Impact
Students with higher attendance percentages achieve better academic results.

Insight:
Class participation remains a critical success factor.


### Behavioral Balance
Productivity depends on balanced lifestyle factors including study time, sleep, and reduced digital distractions.


## Recommendations

### Improve Study Planning
Students should maintain consistent daily study schedules.

### Digital Discipline
Limit phone and social media usage during study periods.

### Promote Healthy Sleep
Educational institutions should encourage proper sleep routines.

### Attendance Monitoring
Schools should implement attendance tracking interventions.
